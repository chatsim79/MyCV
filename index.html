Personal Bio

Hi, I'm Simon and I'm from Northern Ireland, where I grew up during The Troubles. I left to study in Edinburgh from 1998 where I completed a Honours Degree in Mechanical Engineering. After my degree I completed a Chartered Mangers Institute Level 5 Diploma in Management as a 6 month post grad course. More recently I've completed the University of Edinburgh (in association with HyperionDev) fast-track Software Engineering Bootcamp

Once I was armed with bits of paper, I went to work in the oil industry as a Measurement/Logging While Drilling Engineer. This consisted of utilising electronic sensors above the drill bit which read the physical properties of the rock, such as gamma signature, resistivity, density, and porosity. This data was presented as logs (very long graphs), ASCII/LAS data and of course, we used a piece of complex database software to achieve it, which I now suspect was SQL based given the nature of the Query logic. This job took me all over the world, having worked in the U.S., Europe and the Middle East. Below are some photos of me at work.
